LitMix →

LitMix is a new side project which pairs literature & music – two of my deepest interests. I had the idea while reading Nathanel Rich's dystopian novel, Odds Against Tomorrow. The protagonist's incessant and infectious fear of the future read eerily like the sentiments expressed in Arcade Fire's "Modern Man".

As I continued to read, I drew more connections between the the protagonist's bleak outlook and the music I was listening to at the time (perhaps this reveals more about my taste in music than anything).

I decided, then, to more deliberately draw these connections in the form of mixes. Like the mixes I create for friends and family, I curated a list of songs with specific characters and overarching themes in mind.

While I found the book itself underwhelming – the charistmatic characters fall flat in the end – I found the exercise of pairing novel passages with song verses extremely rewarding! The most challenging aspect of curating this mix was pairing songs not only with the content of the book, but with the rest of the mix as well.

I relied largely on songs with largely minor chord progressions; thankfully this is a common characteristic of songs with dark subject matter.
