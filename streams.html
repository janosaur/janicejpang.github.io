LitMix

LitMix is a new side project which pairs literature & music – two of my deepest interests. I had the idea while reading Nathanel Rich's dystopian novel, Odds Against Tomorrow. The protagonist's societal qualms read eerily like lyrics to Arcade Fire's "Modern Man".

As I continued to read, the more connections I drew between the Michael's bleak outlook and the music I listen to – perhaps this reveals more about my taste in music than anything.

Like the mixes I create for friends and family, this first LitMix is carefully curated.

While I found the book itself underwhelming – the charistmatic characters fall flat in the end – I found the exercise of pairing novel passages with song verses extremely rewarding! The most challenging aspect of curating this mix was pairing songs not only with the content of the book, but with the rest of the mix as well.
