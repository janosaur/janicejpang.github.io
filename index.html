Introduction

Hello, internet explorer.

Welcome to Pangcake Land – the digital dwelling of designer Janice Pang. In the real world, Janice lives in the beautiful & bike-friendly city of Davis, California, where she studies Professional Writing and Design at the University of California, Davis. When she's not Photoshoppin' away, she enjoys immersing herself in a good book and some sweet tunes; nothing makes her happier, though, than a live concert.

But back to Pangcake Land. In this world of 1's and 0's, Janice collects the personal and professional work that best represents her playfully calculated approach to design. Go forth & see for yourself.
