Student-Led Seminar: Spring 2015

Principles & Practice of User Experience

The (waitlist) struggle is real.

As a Design major, I feel you. This seminar, however, is firmly capped at 20 people. Unfortunately, I don’t have control over who those 20 people are. The most I can do is strongly suggest that those who do not feel they can dedicate the time to the workload drop the seminar.

If you are 10+ on the waitlist, it is unlikely that you will be able to register for this seminar. If you are 1-10 on the waitlist, I recommend waitlisting only up to Monday, April 13. That Monday, projects will be assigned.

Is auditing an option?

Because this seminar is project-based, I'm maintaining the 20-person cap to ensure students receive equitable attention from both myself and their peers during class. We all know how easy it is to get lost in a sea of faces.

Don't worry, we can still be friends.

I will post the syllabus, schedule, and materials from this seminar on this page as the course progresses; you can follow along from your own computer! I'm also available to chat by appointment.
