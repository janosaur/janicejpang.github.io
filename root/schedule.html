Expectations

Although this is a 1-unit seminar – which, let’s be honest, is not fulfilling any major requirements – you will be expected to uphold the professionalism you would in any other class. This means you are responsible for being aware of and meeting deadlines, and having done the appropriate work in preparation for each week's meeting.

Workload

According to the Carnegie rule (Wiki), you will be expected to complete 3 hours of preparation outside of class each week. If you feel a week’s workload goes beyond this 3 hour expectation, please don’t hesitate to let me know.
