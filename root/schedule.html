Student-Led Seminar: Spring 2015

Principles & Practice of User Experience

Expectations

Although this is a 1-unit seminar – which, let’s be honest, is not fulfilling any major requirements – you will be expected to uphold the professionalism as you would in any other class. This means being aware of and meeting deadlines, and having done the appropriate work for each week in preparation for the following week's meeting.

Workload

According to the Carnegie rule (Wiki), you will be expected to complete 3 hours of preparation outside of class each week. If you feel a week’s workload goes beyond this 3 hour expectation, please don’t hesitate to let me know.
