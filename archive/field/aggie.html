The California Aggie

The California Aggie is the student-run publication of UC Davis and one of the few remaining collegiate broadsheet newspapers in America. During my time at The Aggie, I proudly served as the paper's Design Director, overseeing weekly development and production.

In 2013, the California Media Board awarded The Aggie 2nd place in the annual Best of Collegiate Design.
