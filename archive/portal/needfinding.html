Clarifying Questions

05.15.2015

  1. The pulsing indicated something to Chris, but he wasn’t exactly sure what
  2. Chris began by tapping the the shapes rather than dragging them
  3. He noticed, however, the shapes moved slightly as he tapped them
  4. Chris thought the instructions were part of the background rather than instructional text, so he didn’t read them
  5. Consequently, he didn’t know how to successfully complete the level
  6. Once he read the instructions he was still unable to successfully complete the level
  7. He found the interactions between the shapes more rewarding than completing the level itself
  8. Thus the game mechanism in itself was rewarding
  9. The sound design added to the game’s reward
