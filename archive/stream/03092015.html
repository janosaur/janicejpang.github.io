Designing for...


Since I turned 21 last Saturday, I've been feeling strangely apathetic. Apathetic and incredibly distracted.

My birthday weekend was, for reasons which will later point to my workaholism, kind of stressful.

I'm used to knowing exactly what to do next – what needs to be done and how to get it done. Now, though, I'm just like, "Alright,what's there to do?"

I haven't been particularly interested in talking to anyone, doing anything.

Nothing excites me anymore. Goddamn this is depression. Huh.
