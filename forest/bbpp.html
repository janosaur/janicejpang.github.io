Breaking Bad Pattern Project

When I first began watching the documentary television show Breaking Bad, I couldn't help but notice the vibrant colors and outlandish patterns which contrasted the dark subject matter. As a design nerd, I jumped on the opportunity to take something I love and turn it into something new. Thus, a new project was born: For every episode of Breaking Bad, I would create an original pattern based on an instance within the show.

Complementary Compliment Generator


As an early exercise in this larger user interaction project, I coded a compliment generator in Processing. The user begins by entering his or her name. Acquiring the user-input name, the sketch then generates random adjectives and nouns using arrayed strings to define the user.
