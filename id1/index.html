Student-Led Seminar: Spring 2015

Principles & Practice of User Experience

About the seminar

From the turn signals on the dashboard of your car to the layout of the keys on your keyboard, you have interacted with a designer’s solution to a problem.

This seminar seeks to increase students’ awareness of design in their own environments and to familiarize students with the principles of design thinking. By analyzing design solution case studies; watching talks and reading essays by experienced designers; and applying design thinking to a website redesign; students will assume the role of designer and engage in the design process.

While design thinking can (and should) be applied to all disciplines, this seminar focuses on design thinking through the lens of User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design.
