User-Generated Album Art

In lieu of traditional birthday and holiday presents, I gift hand-selected mixes. Music is something I find intimately experienced and universally relatable. Both to challenge myself and to add a level of interactivity to my mixes, I created an album cover generator in Processing that produces unique artwork for each recipient.

→ Want to create your own album art in Processing? Download the ZIP file!

This project relies on user input (the name and descriptors of the recipient) to produce a visual output (the personalized artwork). And, because this is a music-focused application, the user is serenaded by the swoon-inducing voices of Spiritualized, Cat Power, and Ray LaMontagne throughout their journey.

User-Generated Album Artwork from Janice Pang on Vimeo.

Process

As an early exercise in this larger user interaction project, I coded a compliment generator in Processing. The user begins by entering his or her name. Acquiring the user-input name, the sketch then generates random adjectives and nouns using arrayed strings to define the user.

→ If you'd like to generate compliments of your own, get the Processing code here!
