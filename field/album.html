Made With Love

In lieu of traditional birthday and holiday presents, I gift hand-selected mixes. I'm big on connecting with others through music – something I find both intimately experienced and universally relatable.

To challenge myself & to add a level of interactivity to my mixes, I created an album cover generator in Processing that produces unique artwork for each recipient. This project relies on user input (the name and descriptors of the recipient) to produce a visual output (the personalized artwork).

→ Want to create your own album art in Processing? Download the ZIP file!

Throughout their journey, the user is serenaded by the swoon-inducing voices of Spiritualized, Cat Power, and Ray LaMontagne (courtesy of the Minim audio library).

The video below follows a single sketch of this generative application.

Complementary Compliment Generator


As an early exercise in this larger user interaction project, I coded a compliment generator in Processing. The user begins by entering his or her name. Acquiring the user-input name, the sketch then generates random adjectives and nouns using arrayed strings to define the user.

→ If you'd like to generate compliments of your own, get the Processing code here!
