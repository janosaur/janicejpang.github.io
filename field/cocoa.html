Cocoa Cinnamon

Nestled in the heart of Durham, North Carolina is Cocoa Cinnamon, a brick-and-mortar cafe specializing in coffee, treats, and sipping chocolates. Like Durham, Cocoa Cinnamon prides itself in being a creative space for its eclectic and industrial community.
