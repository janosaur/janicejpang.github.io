Simplify

Our lives are shaped by decisions – some of which may be as intuitive and inconsequential as what we'll eat for lunch; others as difficult and influential as choosing the right college.

Following the mantra "Make the Complex Simple", I prototyped a decision-making web application called Simplify. This app attempts to dismantle the thought processes behind human decisions.
