KDVS Fundraiser

KDVS (90.3 FM) is the freeform community radio station of Davis, CA. Every April, the station hosts a fundraiser to keep great content — from 2am death metal to intimate house shows — pumping year-round.

This year, the station celebrated its DIY radio roots with a "Homegrown Radio" theme. When we launched, we saw a significant increase in donations from years past – passing the $60,000 goal in less than 3 weeks!

→ Explore the mobile prototype!

As the lead designer, I developed the fundraiser's visual and interactive system. Developer Ricardo Matsui and I teamed with KDVS to implement the system throughout the mobile experience.
