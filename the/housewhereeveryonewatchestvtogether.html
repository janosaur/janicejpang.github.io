Breaking Bad Pattern Project

Design nerd

When I first began watching the documentary television show Breaking Bad, I couldn't help but notice the vibrant colors and outlandish patterns which contrasted the dark subject matter. I jumped on the opportunity to take something I love and turn it into something new. Thus, a new project was born: For every episode of Breaking Bad, I would create an original pattern based on an instance within the show.
