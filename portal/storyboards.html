05.15.2015

This 5-panel storyboard tells the story of 9-year old Kelvin and his 36-year old mother Alex. Kelvin is extremely interested in science and Alex wants to support Kelvin's interests.

Alex brings Kelvin to the Art Ark, which she knows is a combination of color, nature, and science. At the Art Ark, Kelvin encounters the interactive color game, which prompts him to choose the right color of the leaves in the picture. Kelvin chooses the incorrect answer, and the game, rather than chiding him, explains to Kelvin why the leaves are actually green. In this way, Kelvin gains a deeper understanding of color and how it plays into his perception of his environment.
